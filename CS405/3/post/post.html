Types of Hackers

The black-hat hacking group LulzSec first gained recognition when they used an SQL injection attack to steal information about millions of user accounts from Sony Pictures Europe. Following the Sony Pictures Europe breach, LulzSec published the names, birth dates, addresses, emails, phone numbers and passwords of thousands of people who had entered contests promoted by Sony, and publicly boasted of its exploits (Mahdawi, 2012). The article goes on to say the cost of the breach (probably in compliance fines). This case study highlights the importance of proper SQL query handling in order to protect personal user data.
