In my experience, the most effective way to gather requirements is by first understanding the environment within which you are working. After that, you will be able to better understand stakeholders' requirements. Once you have that basic understanding, it is important to hold as many meetings as it takes in order to answer all of the foreseeable questions you might have. Of course it is also important to have others from your team join these meetings and remember that if you have questions in the future to reach back out to the stakeholders.
