I think contemporary culture has had significant impact on creative expression and the humanities. One way in which contemporary culture and modern technology has impacted creative expression is through digital books. Thanks to Amazon much of the world now consumes its literature digitally. Subscriptions such as kindle unlimited allows for book reading to be affordable, those who may not have been able to read new books outside of what their library had available now can read the latest books from their favorite authors as soon as its released. Amazon has also given many authors who previously may not have had the resources or opportunities to have their book published more options as well. Amazon has a program that allows authors to self publish their books digitally through amazon, this allows more authors to get their creations out into society and allows for more diverse literary material to be more easily accessible.
