The design option I selected was to develop an app that can be used by concerned family members of a person with dementia that want to be alerted if their relative should walk more than 1,000 steps from home, or leaves their stove on for more than 60 minutes. The main purpose of this application is to help ensure the safety of the relative by informing family members of hazards that could jeopardize that safety and require a welfare check. The application will allow multiple family members to monitor the same relative and provide each family member the ability to customize their alerts and notifications.
